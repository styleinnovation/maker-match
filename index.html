OUR TOOLKIT


We leverage design thinking to build empathy for our guests, identify their implicit and explicit needs, and ideate new product/services to meet those needs. With our understanding of the competitive landscape and guests needs, we create a differentiated, winning strategy represented in a strategic choice cascade. To minimize the risk of innovation, we use a continuous cycle of learning, building and measuring the various concepts in low-fidelity, rapid tests using the Lean Startup Methodology. Based on what we learn, we make recommendations on whether to buy, build or partner to turn being those businesses to market as quickly as possible.


OUR APPROACH TO INNOVATION

We believe in connecting with our guest throughout the innovation process. We start with guest immersion and iterate within each phase, with a continued focus on testing our assumptions. We have a bias towards action because "it's easier to act your way into a new way of thinking, than think your way into a new way of acting". At each phase, we ask key questions to ensure we are learning and reducing risk.
